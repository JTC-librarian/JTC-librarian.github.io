Community Involvement and Service


I am an active member of the national and international ExLibris User Groups. I have contributed to the community by, among other things, posting on the ExLibris Tech blog (on implementing the RefME Cite Button, 2018; on a simple Python Label Printer, 2024) and sharing ideas on the ExLibris Ideas Exchange (my suggestion to allow export of citations by batch from saved items has been accepted and put into development by ExLibris.
